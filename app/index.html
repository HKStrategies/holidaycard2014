Snow Fall

In 2014 Hill+Knowlton Strategies joined nobel laureates and people just like you to work toward completing One Billion Acts of Peace. We each found our own peace in the process and wish you peace this holiday season and beyond.

In 2014, Hill+Knowlton Strategies be came a founding partner of PeaceJam Foundation's One Billion Acts of Peace (1BAOP), a campaign to inspire people around the globe to help tackle the toughest issues facing humanity.

The multi-year program leverages Nobel Peace Laureates including Archbishop Desmond Tutu and the Dali Lama to recruit people around the globe to complete one billion acts of peace. Our firm has been supporting 1BAOP by donating our time and talents to support the campaign with strategic counsel, media relations and event coordination. As co-creaters of 1BAOP, H+K U.S. offices, practices and colleagues have worked together to log "acts of peace" in the march to 1 billion.

H+K New York Volunteers Decorate Costumes for Hospitalized Children

On September 29, 2014, H+K volunteers used their imagination to help decorate costume hats and medallions at the New York office as part of an Only Make Believe (OMB) event to help supply costumes for hospitalized children to wear during OMB performances. Only Make Believe (OMB) is a non-profit organization that brings the joy of theater to kids in hospitals around New York City and Washington DC.
